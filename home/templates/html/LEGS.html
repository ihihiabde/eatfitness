Quadriceps

Muscle located on the front of the thigh which has the particularity of being the most massive in the human body since it is responsible for supporting most of its weight and allows movement.

Hamstring

Your hamstring muscles are skeletal muscles at the back of your thigh. You use them to walk, climb stairs, do squats and perform many other leg movements. Hamstring injuries are the most common sports injury. Stretching, warming up and not pushing through pain in your hip, knee and leg are the best ways to avoid injuring your hamstrings.

Calf muscles

Stretching the calf muscle past its normal length results in tearing of some calf muscle fibers. Calf muscle strains can vary from mild (slight pain) to severe (complete tear of the calf muscle).
